cross_domain_network

Created co-occurrence networks for each corn genotype to examine positive and negative correlations between taxa and look for differential associations between the two networks. In order to see what fungal or bacterial taxa are associated with Tox4 and K49, 16S and ITS abundances were combined into a cross domain network. To limit the size of the network, ASVs were limited to those that were present in at least 30% of the samples. This resulted in 84 bacterial ASVs and 15 fungal ASVs . Association matrices were created using SpiecEasi with the Meinshausen-Bhlmann neighborhood selection algorithm and then passed to NetCoMis netConstruct function to conduct a network comparison between the two groups.
